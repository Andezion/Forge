What FORGE Offers

📊

Advanced Analytics

Track progress with sophisticated metrics — strength coefficient, volume analysis, workout frequency, intensity, and consistency. Visualize your gains with interactive charts powered by proven formulas.

🤖 AI-Powered

AI Workout Generation

Generate personalized workouts using LLaMA 3.3-70B via Groq. The AI adapts to your training direction — powerlifting, street lifting, armwrestling, or full body — and fits your exercise library.

🍎 New

Nutrition Planning

AI-driven nutrition algorithms tailored to your goals. Get macronutrient breakdowns, calorie targets, and meal timing recommendations based on your training profile and body weight history.

🏆 Social

Leaderboards & Challenges

Compete with the global community through real-time leaderboards. Create and join challenges, track your rank, and push your limits against other serious athletes.

🧠

Muscle Recovery Tracker

Know exactly which muscle groups are ready to train. The tracker analyzes your full workout history and muscle group assignments to show optimal recovery status and timing for every muscle.

📅

Smart Calendar

Visual workout history with streak tracking. Exercises with fewer reps than last time are highlighted in orange — spot regression instantly. Skipped exercises shown in red so nothing falls through the cracks.

💪

Personal Records

Track your 1RM for every exercise and compare against world records. See exactly how your lifts stack up against the best in the world, updated from a curated world records database.

🫀 New

Wellness Tracking

Log sleep quality, stress levels, and energy. Correlate wellness data with workout performance for a complete picture of your recovery and readiness to train.

👥 Social

Friends & Community

Add friends, view their training profiles and stats, and stay connected. The social layer keeps you accountable and motivated through community engagement and friendly competition.

🎯

Progression Insights

Data-driven analysis of your long-term progression. Identify plateaus, detect imbalances, optimize training load, and get actionable insights based on your actual performance data.

⚖️

Body Weight History

Complete weight tracking history integrated with analytics. Correlate body composition changes with strength progression to understand your body's response to training over time.

🎨

Full Customization

Personalize your profile with custom avatars, animated frames, and theme colors. Build your unique training identity. Achievements and badges unlock as you hit milestones.

// artificial intelligence

AI Integration

🤖

Powered by LLaMA 3.3-70B via Groq

FORGE integrates Groq's ultra-fast inference API to run Meta's LLaMA 3.3-70B model. The AI layer provides workout generation, quality evaluation, nutrition planning, and smart training recommendations — all personalized to your training direction, exercise library, and performance history. Configure your own Groq API key in the app settings.

⚡

Instant Generation

Generate complete, structured workouts in seconds. The AI considers your training direction, available exercises, and fitness profile to create optimal session plans ready to execute.

🔍

Workout Evaluation

Get AI-powered feedback on your completed workouts. Quality assessments, intensity analysis, and improvement suggestions based on your logged performance and training history.

📈

Smart Recommendations

Recovery-aware workout suggestions that factor in muscle fatigue, wellness entries, and training history to recommend the most effective and appropriate next session.

How It Works

👤

1. Create Profile

Sign up with Firebase authentication. Set your body weight, training direction, experience level, and connect your Groq API key to unlock AI features.

📋

2. Choose Program

Select from curated training programs, build a custom plan, or use AI generation to instantly create a personalized workout tailored to your goals.

🏋️

3. Track Workouts

Log every set, rep, and weight in real-time during your session. Analytics, volume, and progress metrics are calculated automatically as you train.

🔬

4. Analyze & Improve

Review progress charts, check AI insights, monitor muscle recovery, track wellness, and optimize your training based on comprehensive data-driven analysis.
